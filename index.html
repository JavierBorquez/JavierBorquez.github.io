My Background / About Me
I got my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at the Universidad de Santiago de Chile, then joined the SIA Lab in the Fall of 2021 as a PhD student; My current research interests lay in the designing and deployment of robots that leverage safety techniques to closely interact with humans.
In my free time I like to practice Taekwon-do, volunteer helping cats and building stuff out of discarded electronics.
